## Setup your account

Follow these steps to start using EaziWage:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your account at [app.eaziwage.com](https://app.eaziwage.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete KYC">
    Upload your verification documents for approval
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Payment Method">
    Link your mobile money account or bank account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Wages">
    Request an advance once your employer is onboarded
  </Step>
</Steps>
