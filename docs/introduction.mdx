## Welcome to EaziWage

EaziWage is a comprehensive earned wage access platform that enables employees to access their earned wages before payday while providing employers with advanced payroll management, risk assessment, and compliance tools across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Instant Wage Access">
    Employees can access up to 50% of their earned wages at any time during the pay period, with funds disbursed via mobile money or bank transfer.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Multi-Country Support">
    Operating across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda with support for local mobile money providers and banking systems.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Advanced Risk Management">
    Built-in fraud detection, KYC verification, and credit scoring to protect both employers and employees.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Seamless Integration">
    Easy integration with existing payroll systems and HRIS platforms via our REST API.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
